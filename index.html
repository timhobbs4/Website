D'ALRAE SOLUTIONS

D’alRae Solutions is a privately owned Brisbane-based IT Solutions Consultancy Firm, with its Network reaching far and wide, offering both on-site and remote working arrangements.

We are a partner in the SAP PartnerEdge open ecosystem. As an SAP partner, we specialise in various SAP-based platforms and technologies, along with other supporting technologies. Our resources are experienced in both Technical and Functional Consulting, along with Project Managers and Business Analysts. All of whom deliver an engaging Customer experience, both in a professional and flexible manner.

Extensive Industry experience ranges from Fresh Produce to Government, Mining to Retail.

D’alRae really is an exciting and highly sought after IT Solutions Consultancy Firm, offering best practice solutions with the Customer always at the forefront of all decision making.

Delivery flexibility with the cloud
Cloud is no longer an emerging technology or a “special IT project” use case. According to McKinsey*, enterprises are now in the middle of a fundamental shift from building IT to consuming IT and are planning to transition IT workloads at a significant rate and pace to a Hybrid Cloud infrastructure.

What has changed though, is the preferred mix of Cloud and on-premises solutions, management and services. It’s now all about a hybrid approach, choosing the right options, in the right way, to optimise the use of your technology and resources and maximising the value of your Cloud investments.
