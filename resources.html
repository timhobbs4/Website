Resourcing - Find the right person for your requirements

The people you need for your business

People are at the heart of every successful business initiative and we know that sourcing, placing and retaining quality technical resources can be challenging. At D'alrae Solutions, we know talent and are able to assist you in attracting and retaining the absolute best fit of talent to advance any organisation’s goals.

D’alRae Solutions Team Members are experienced in working with Federal Government Departments, notably on the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) Project, who contributed towards successful a Angular Project. The D’alRae Team consists of skilled developers who have the proven ability to either work autonomously or within a Project Team Environment to drive towards a successful delivery.

D'alRae specialises in providing contract and staff augmentation services across a range of industries.
These services include:
• sourcing of skilled resources across multiple platforms,
• candidate assessment and placement,
• onboarding,
• induction, and
• post placement management.

Contracting

We speed the time to productivity for our placements by facilitating a smooth onboarding programme. We have an in-depth understanding of the technical skills and competencies you need to get the full value from your technology investment. For contract positions, we also remain in close contact with the client and our consultant, proactively facilitating employee engagement, performance, and retention throughout each assignment.

Staff Augmentation

Our team has a broad range of expertise to suit your business needs, including:
• Business Analysts,
• Data Analysts,
• SAP Functional Consultants,
• ABAP Developers,
• Full Stack Developers, and
• Fiori Developers with extensive Angular knowledge.
The Team are skilled in following a standard Development Life Cycle, as well as delivering in both Waterfall and Agile methodologies, making the D’alRae Team a standout in its field.
